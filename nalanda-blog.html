Nalanda Counselling Centre for Higher Education

Over time, Nalanda Academy has acquired valuable insights into the challenges confronting our students. Additionally, we have developed resources and networks to address these challenges effectively. With the establishment of the Nalanda Counselling Centre and its committed team of mentors, we extend access to all our resources and networks to students from underprivileged backgrounds, empowering them to pursue higher education.

Equitable Access to Higher Education

The primary objective of the Counselling Centre is to bridge the gap in access to higher education for students from underprivileged backgrounds. With a team of over 200 mentors, we provide personalized guidance, empowering our students to achieve their educational goals.

Holistic Support System

The Counselling Centre endeavors to foster a holistic support system that not only assists students in overcoming academic hurdles but also addresses their socio-economic challenges. through tailored counselling sessions.

Empowerment through Networks

By leveraging our established connections and partnerships, the Centre aims to provide students with opportunities for research, internships, and other experiential learning, enriching their educational experience.
