Support Us

Become a Nalanda Mitra

Nalanda offers all training and facilities to the students absolutely free of cost and is run entirely on the financial support from friends and well-wishers. We need support for our two causes. You can choose either or both the causes to support. You can make one time donation or can choose to become a monthly donor. At present we can take donations only from Indian citizens. All our donors are eligible to get 50 % tax-exemption under section 80 G of the Income Tax Act.

Your financial support allows us to do what we do best – to train our students to study at the top universities in India and abroad for free.

Support Nalanda Abhiyan Campus

Currently our centre has just one classroom and no hostel or mess facilities to offer. Now we are constructing our residential campus at Waifad, Wardha. In the first phase, we are building 3 classrooms and residential facilities for 100 students with a total cost of about 5 Crores.

You can support us in building a safe and encouraging space for our students to learn and excel. To know more, click here
