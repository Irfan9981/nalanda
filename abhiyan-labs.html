Empowering to Innovation

At Nalanda Abhiyan Labs, we're dedicated to nurturing talent and sparking innovation. Through our free training programs, we empower students to dive into various technologies like Scratch, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and more.

Interdisciplinary Projects

We love projects that blend different subjects! Our students work on exciting ventures like Face Recognition, Smart Headgear, and Gesture-Controlled Arms, using tools like 3D printers. This helps them understand concepts better and apply them in real-life situations.

Community Engagement

We believe in sharing knowledge with everyone! That's why we've conducted over 50 school workshops and 3 summer workshops, training over 2000 school children in emerging technologies. Join us at Nalanda Abhiyan Labs and let's create, innovate, and inspire together!

Nalanda Abhiyan Labs, situated at the Samyak Buddha Vihara in Wardha, stands as a dynamic center where creativity converges with technology. Founded in 2018 by the visionary Late Abhiyan Humane, our labs provide a distinctive space for students to explore the captivating intersections of Art, Science, and Technology.

At Nalanda Abhiyan Labs, we are committed to nurturing talent and inspiring innovation. Through our free training programs, students are empowered to engage with a spectrum of technologies, including Scratch, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, sensors, drones, and physical computation. Our objective is to equip young minds with the skills essential for success in today’s digital era.

Science & Technology Workshops for School Students

Artist-in-Residence

A hallmark of Nalanda Abhiyan Labs is our emphasis on interdisciplinary projects. We encourage students to embark on projects that traverse multiple disciplines, enabling them to deepen their understanding and practical application of concepts. Noteworthy projects include Face Recognition, Smart Headgear, Gesture- Controlled Arm, and the utilization of 3D Printers.

Community engagement lies at the core of our endeavors. Having conducted over 50 school workshops and 3 summer workshops, the Labs has trained more than 2000 school children in emerging technologies.
