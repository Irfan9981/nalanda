Digital Nalanda

Launched in October 2020 to support Nalanda students' learning during the COVID-19 pandemic, Digital Nalanda is a free online learning platform that makes education accessible to all.

12 Online Schools

These cover a diverse array of subjects and domain-specific knowledge ranging from Data Science to Buddhist Studies to Communication & Media Studies.

Preparatory Courses

Digital Nalanda offers specialized online courses tailored for rural, non-English medium students to prepare for entrance exams for premier Indian universities.

Mentorship Programs

Digital Nalanda provides mentorship to students interested in pursuing education in the fields of STEM, as well as to those interested in pursuing higher education abroad.
