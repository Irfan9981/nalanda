Nalanda Academy

Nalanda Academy, located at Samyak Buddha Vihar in Wardha, follows the vision of anti-caste leaders like Jotiba Phule, Savitrimai Phule, and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar. It offers free training, guidance, and mentorship to underprivileged students seeking higher education. The Academy conducts an annual, 10-month Foundation Course providing free residential training to over 200 underprivileged students, enabling them to pursue higher education.  Over the past decade, it has helped over 1600 students from 15 states secure admission to prestigious higher education institutions across the country, with 37 students currently enrolled in renowned foreign universities.To sustain its operations, the Academy relies on financial support from individuals committed to the Ambedkarite philosophy of empowering communities through education. 

Nalanda Journey

2013

Nalanda's journey began in 2013 with a four-month training program led by Anoop Kumar at Dr. Ambedkar College of Social Work, benefiting 22 undergraduate women students.

The local community welcomes Nalanda to operate from Samyak Buddha Vihar, where it has been based since, providing facilities for over 200 students.
